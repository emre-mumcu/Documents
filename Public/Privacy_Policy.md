This Privacy Policy describes how MiCard ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information. By using MiCard, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use
MiCard is designed to be a personal application.

Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) to external servers. Any information you enter into the app (such as name, phone number, or email) is stored locally on your device only.

Device Permissions: The app may request basic permissions (such as internet access to open social media links). We do not use these permissions to track you or access your private files.

Note: If you use third-party links within the app (e.g., a link to your LinkedIn profile), those external sites have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these third-party sites.

3. Data Security
Since MiCard does not collect or store data on remote servers, your data is as secure as your physical device. We recommend using standard device security measures (passcodes, biometric locks) to protect the information stored on your phone.

4. Children’s Privacy
MiCard does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children.
